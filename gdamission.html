Mission Statement for the Golden Dialetik

Transcendent is an overused term, but in truth, for real peace to occur, people need the courage to transcend their own boundaries:

"Your perception of the One needs to grow beyond any thought you have ever had about the Dialetik, by simultaneously seeing the Golden Dialetik as the One, as God, as Tao, as intelligent and mechanical design. These are all true and by embracing this irony one can get deeper within the Golden Dialetik." (The Golden Dialetik, p. 186-187)

This is not a transcendence of a bodily nature, but a true transcendence, a metaphysical transcendence. This concept is at the heart of my considerations, and my principal faith is that this precept from the Golden Dialetik can lead to world peace.

The primary mission of those people, who understand and are dedicated to this concept and the concepts embodied in the Golden Dialetik, is to promote unfettered education and support further understanding of the Golden Dialetik, based on the belief that greater understanding of this phenomenon will foster greater understanding between the peoples of the world.
